Individual Therapy

I may be a good fit for you if you are navigating trauma, anxiety, depression, shame resilience, life transitions, low self esteem and relational difficulties. It is an honor to walk with individuals through the many challenges of various stages of life.

I help individuals to express and process struggles and stress's in a safe environment. I come alongside you to provide support as I help you in developing healthier rhythms in your life.

How I Can Help

I can help you to cope with many of life's challenges. I'm a Marriage and Family Therapist in the Louisville Kentucky area, and I'm here to support you.

Couples Therapy

My hope is to help couples to understand more about the patterns that keep you feeling stuck. I give couples tools to engage with their emotions, needs, and longings so they can reach for their partners in new ways that create a safe and secure bond. My focus areas with couples are, pre-marital, marriage enrichment, communication issues, and recovering from infidelity.

Having experienced brokenness and healing in her own journey, Haley considers it a great honor to walk with others as they work through their process of individual healing. She strives to use an integrative approach in order to foster a non-judgmental and compassionate environment for clients to explore and grow in each of their journeys. Haley values creating safe space for each client to explore their path to healing.

Haley is a member of the American Association for Marriage and Family Therapists (AAMFT). She is Trained in Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (Level II) and certified in Prepare/Enrich. Her License number is 27859

Getting Started

Starting therapy can be an intimidating experience, but it's also a brave step towards healing and growth. Here's what you can expect:

  1. Initial Contact
  2. Phone Consultation: We may connect via phone so I can get a sense of what you're looking for in therapy and answer any initial questions you may have.
  3. Scheduling an Appointment: If you feel comfortable moving forward, you'll schedule your first appointment. I will provide information about my availability, fees, and what to expect in your first session.
  4. First Session: The first session is typically an intake session where I gather more detailed information about your concerns, background, and goals for therapy. You'll also discuss logistics like scheduling, fees, and confidentiality. This is a good time to ask any questions you may have about the therapeutic process.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are online sessions like?

I use a HIPAA compliant platform. You are not required to download any new software. I will send you a link to your appointment and you can click on that link to enter the meeting. If you are wanting to use your phone for the online session then you would need to download the TeleHealth by Simple Practice app.

If finding privacy in your house is a barrier please mention that to me and we can talk about various options.

How much is a therapy session?

A 45-50 minute session is $125.

What can I expect starting therapy to be like?

For our first meeting I will want to learn more about you. We will talk about what is bringing you in and set goals for what kind of change you'd like to see. I use this information to tailor therapy so that our following sessions are the most helpful for your needs. The following sessions will be based on your goals.

What forms of payment do you accept?

I accept credit card (including VISA, MASTERCARD, AMEX and DISCOVER), cash, check and HSA or FSA cards.

Do you accept insurance?

I am not currently accepting insurance. Insurance requires the diagnosis and release of all details in your medical record, which can sometimes limit the care you might need. For these reasons I am not accepting insurance. I am able to accept HSA and FSA cards. I offer receipts for clients to file for possible reimbursement with their insurance company.
